Pricing FAQs

Common questions about our pricing

Are there any hidden fees?

+

No. Our pricing is transparent. The only additional costs are reimbursable expenses (like hospital bills or materials) which we document with receipts.

How do I pay for services?

+

We accept international bank transfers, PayPal, and mobile money (M-Pesa). Payment terms are outlined in your service agreement.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

+

Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled with 30 days notice. We believe in earning your trust every month.

What if the task costs more than estimated?

+

We always get your approval before incurring additional costs. Our estimates are accurate based on experience, and we communicate proactively about any changes.
