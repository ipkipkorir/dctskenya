Our Story

Diaspora Care & Trust Services (DCTS Kenya) was born from a simple observation: Kenyans living abroad face a persistent and common challenge. They are responsible for the well-being of their families, properties, and obligations back home, yet they lack reliable, trustworthy, and professional local support.

Whether managing parents' health, supervising construction projects, resolving land issues, or ensuring children's education, many diaspora Kenyans depend on informal networks of friends, relatives, or hired agents. These informal arrangements often result in mismanagement, emotional stress, and loss of trust.

We recognized this gap and decided to do something about it. DCTS Kenya was established to provide a professional, accountable, and structured solutionâ€”replacing uncertainty with peace of mind for diaspora families across the globe.
